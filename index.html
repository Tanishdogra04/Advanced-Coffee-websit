About Us

Welcome to our cozy corner of coffee culture, nestled in the serene hills of Dharamshala, Himachal Pradesh. At Coffee by Tanish Dogra, we believe that every cup should tell a story — of passion, warmth, and community. Founded with love for both rich brews and peaceful mountain life, our mission is to bring people together over the perfect blend. From handpicked beans to handcrafted beverages, we’re committed to quality, sustainability, and unforgettable flavor. Whether you’re a traveler, a local, or just a coffee lover, you're always welcome at our table.
